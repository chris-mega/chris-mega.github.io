IROS 2019

Robot Magic and Music competition in Macau!

Christian Melendez-Gallegos
Sep 27, 2019
Polaris [Left] and Oscar [Right]

Participation with team Snobots for University of Manitoba in IROS 2019 in Macau. We programmed two robots to play music together with a human!

Leaned more about OpenCV, visual servoing, inverse kinematics, UDP connections, human-robot-interaction and music theory.

Qualification video

We got 2nd place among 6 teams! The prize was a hand sponsored by Seed Robotics
